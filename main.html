As the nation’s combat logistics support agency, the Defense Fuel manages the global supply chain –
from raw materials to end user to disposition – for the Army, Navy, Air Force,CRPF, Coast Guard,
10 combatant commands,
other government agencies, and partner and allied nations. DF is a global enterprise –
wherever the Republic of India has a significant military presence, DF is there to support.
